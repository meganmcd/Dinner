About Us

A casual fine dining restaurant, offering eclectic American cuisine and warm hospitality in a rustic historically preserved building. A high, open beam ceiling, exposed brick and concrete floors display the original space, while an open kitchen, banquette seating and gentle lighting contribute to an intimate and contemporary feel.

Our food is sourced from top quality seasonal ingredients, featuring a high percentage of local and organic products. Our menu changes every six to eight weeks, offering a mix of new and signature dishes.

Meg's Dinner specializes in breakfast all-day, lunch, dinner, desserts and specially crafted teas and coffees. Meg's Dinner takes pride in its artisan offerings and also offering catering services.

Guest Bartender Night!

Every month, we host a local charity for Guest Bartender Night. It’s a fun event for charities to earn donations for their cause. Each charity brings their own flavor to the event… and gets to create a special cocktail for the evening.
